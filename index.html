Travel Safety

The quiet,
essential bit.

General advice that applies almost everywhere. City-specific notes live inside each guide.

Documents

Passport valid 6+ months past return. Keep a photo of your passport, insurance, and key bookings in a separate cloud folder you can reach without your phone.

Money

Carry two cards from different networks. Tell your bank you're travelling. Always have ~SGD 100 cash equivalent for taxis when card terminals fail.

Health

Pack a small kit: paracetamol, anti-diarrheal, rehydration salts, plasters, antihistamines. Get jabs done at least 4 weeks before departure.

Insurance

Non-negotiable. Confirm coverage for adventure activities, scooter riding, and any pre-existing conditions before you fly.

Scams

Common patterns: "closed temple" detours, broken meter taxis, jet-ski damage claims, friendly strangers at ATMs. When in doubt, walk away.

Emergencies

Save the local emergency number and your country's embassy contact in your phone before you arrive. Share your itinerary with one person back home.
